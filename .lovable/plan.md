
# UX Designer Portfolio — Bold & Creative Style

## Pages & Sections (Single-page scrollable layout)

### 1. Hero Section
- Large bold headline with your name and title (placeholder: "Jane Doe — UX Designer")
- Short tagline about your design philosophy
- Vibrant gradient background with animated accent shapes
- CTA button: "View My Work" (scrolls to projects)

### 2. About Me
- Brief bio paragraph highlighting your experience
- Skills/tools showcase with bold icon badges: **Figma, Zeplin, Adobe XD, Sketch**, etc.
- Years of experience counter with bold typography

### 3. Featured Projects (3 Client Showcases)
- Card-based grid layout with hover animations
- Each card includes: project thumbnail placeholder, client name, project description, your role, key deliverables
- Bold color accents per project card
- "View Case Study" button on each (scrolls to detail or opens modal)

### 4. Services / What I Do
- Icon-based grid: UI Design, UX Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Design Systems
- Short descriptions under each

### 5. Contact Me
- Contact form with Name, Email, and Message fields
- Display email address and phone number prominently
- Social links (LinkedIn, Dribbble placeholders)
- Bold CTA: "Let's Work Together"

### 6. Sticky Navigation
- Top nav bar with smooth scroll links: About, Projects, Services, Contact
- Download Resume button

## Design Direction
- Bold color palette with vibrant accents (electric blue, coral, deep purple)
- Large typography, strong visual hierarchy
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects
- Fully responsive for mobile/tablet/desktop
